Overview

This privacy policy describes what kinds of information are collected from you, and how we use your information.

This website does not collect personal details, or track you with any technology. Some third parties used by the website engage in tracking, and will be listed separately.

Personal information

No personal information is required to use the website, with or without an account. Registration collects a valid email address, country, website, and business name. These details are only made public in the case that you choose to publish a testimonial, which will be visible on the frontpage to any other user.

The information collected is not never shared or sold to anyone. It is only used for business communication and voluntary testimonials.

If you wish to obtain a copy of all information we hold about you, or delete your account to purge all of your information from our servers, please send a message via the contact page.

Tracking

This website does not engage in tracking. No statistics are collected, in any form, including anonymized.

Third parties

This website makes use of Stripe, a payment processor. This involves collection of payment information from you, as well as analytics which track you as you use the website for the purposes of fraud prevention.

No payment information collected by Stripe is made available to this website, as part of Stripe's PCI compliance. For full details, consult Stripe's privacy policy available here.

The newsletter functionality makes use of Mailchimp, a mailing service. They store your email address for the purposes of delivering the mailing service only, including the ability to cancel at any time. To the best of our knowledge Mailchimp does not engage in sharing or selling your email address. For full details, consult Mailchimp's privacy policy available here.
