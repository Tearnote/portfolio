Overview

These terms detail the obligations of the website as a service provider, and website users as clients.

In broad terms, the service provider provides a commissioning service, allowing clients to request work from the service provider in exchange for compensation.

Records on this website do not constitute a form of contract. A contract between service provider and client will be signed separately via direct email communication as part of standard procedure.

Service provider's conduct

The service provider is obliged to respond to client requests. Requests will be validated and discussed out-of-band, via direct email communication.

Once a request is accepted and paid for, the service provider will provide timely updates on commission progress, and will continue to be responsive to the client's opinions and requests for minor alterations.

Note that the terms in this document are not legally binding, and only constitute a good-will agreement. The document does not mean to replace or supersede any legal agreements made between service provider and client.

Client's conduct

The client must use the website for its intended purpose. The contact functionality must not be abused for queries not related to the service being provided. Requested commissions must also be relevant to the skillset demonstrated via project showcase on the frontpage.

Any attempt to gain unauthorized access to administrative features, or making use of any clearly unintended functionality such as bugs and exploits, gives the service provider the right to block the client's access to the website permanently.
